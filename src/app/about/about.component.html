This demo is built on Angular platform and written fully in TypeScript

As a component set it utilizes the power of brilliant Angular Material and its awesome extension - Covalent

Then flexbox via Angular Flex Layout and finally NGX Charts - a free charting engine

This demo may look trivial, but in fact it's based on quite complicated techniques like
webworkers, async\await & RxJS streams, directives & parametrized animations.

It's very much in progress, but of course there's something to show for now:

  • Real-time sorting algorithm comparison
  • Simple fractal painter
